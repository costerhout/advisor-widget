                    The Western Undergraduate Exchange (WUE) program is designed to help undergraduates who live in western states pursue higher education at a public college or university outside their home state, but within the western region at an affordable cost.

                    WUE states include: Alaska, Arizona, California, Colorado, Hawaii, Idaho, Montana, Nevada, New Mexico, North Dakota, Oregon, South Dakota, Utah, Washington, Wyoming and the U.S. Pacific territories.

                    Note: Not all UAS degree programs are eligible for WUE tuition.
